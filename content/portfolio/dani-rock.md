Dani and Rock's wedding at Magnolia Utah was a celebration of elegance and warmth. Their floral vision called for lush white and ivory blooms accented with flowing greenery — timeless, romantic, and full of texture. Cottage Floral Design served as ceremony concept lead and floral designer for their full wedding day.

From the ceremony arch to the sweetheart table to the bridal party bouquets, every arrangement was crafted to feel abundant and intentional. The vintage Heritage Fleet car was given its own floral moment, and the temple ceremony florals carried the same softness throughout the day.

This was a true team effort with an incredible group of vendors — from the planning expertise of @merrimacweddings to the stunning photography and videography by @shalesedeserayphotography. We are honored to have been part of Dani and Rock's day.
