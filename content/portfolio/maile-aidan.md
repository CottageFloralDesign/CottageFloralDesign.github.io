Maile and Aidan's wedding at The Northampton House was a summer celebration filled with light, love, and lush florals. Their palette of white, cream, and abundant greenery created an airy and romantic atmosphere throughout the ceremony and reception spaces.

Cottage Floral Design handled floral design and décor coordination for the full day — from Maile's flowing bridal bouquet to the ceremony arch to the reception centerpieces that anchored each table with beauty. Every arrangement was designed to feel organic and full, celebrating the natural elegance of the blooms.

Video and photography by @k.t.productions_ captured the warmth of the day perfectly. We loved collaborating with this wonderful couple and their vendor team to create a celebration that felt uniquely them.
