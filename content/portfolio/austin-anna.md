Anna and Austin's proposal at the Homestead Resort in Midway, Utah was a moment designed to take her breath away — and the florals were crafted to match. Ivory, cream, and soft blush blooms created an atmosphere of romance and intimacy against the backdrop of the stunning resort grounds.

Cottage Floral Design handled the coordination, content, and florals for this surprise proposal. Every arrangement was designed to feel lush and effortless, drawing the eye while letting the moment between Anna and Austin take center stage.

Photography by @amandaallisonphoto captured the magic of the day beautifully. We are so grateful to have been part of this special milestone for this lovely couple.
