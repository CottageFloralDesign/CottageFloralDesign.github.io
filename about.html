Our Story

About Us

Meet the Designer

Hello, I'm the Heart
Behind the Blooms

Owner & Lead Floral Designer

What began as a love for gathering wildflowers from my grandmother's garden blossomed into a lifelong passion for floral artistry. I founded Cottage Floral Design with a simple belief: flowers should feel like they were just gathered from the most beautiful garden you've ever wandered through.

Every arrangement I create is guided by the rhythms of the seasons, the unique character of each bloom, and the story you want to tell on your most special day. I believe in the beauty of imperfection — the slightly unfurling rose, the delicate tendril, the unexpected pop of texture.

When I'm not elbow-deep in petals, you'll find me exploring farmers' markets, tending my own cutting garden, or dreaming up new color palettes with a cup of tea in hand.

Our Philosophy

What We Believe

Seasonal Beauty

We design with what the earth offers each season, creating arrangements that feel authentic, lush, and alive with natural character.

Personal Connection

Your flowers should be as unique as your love story. We listen deeply and design with intention, ensuring every petal reflects you.

Artful Imperfection

Locally Grown

From Field
to Celebration

Whenever possible, we source our blooms from local growers and farmers who share our commitment to sustainable, thoughtful cultivation. It means fresher flowers, less waste, and supporting the community we call home.
